About Us

TPGIT Engineering College was established in the year 2010 with the vision of offering world-class engineering education to rural and urban students. The college is located in a peaceful environment at Bagayam, Vellore, providing a perfect atmosphere for learning and growth.

We aim to produce technically strong, socially responsible, and ethically sound engineers.

Vision

To become a center of excellence in engineering education and research, producing globally competent professionals.

Mission

  • To provide quality education with modern teaching methods
  • To promote innovation, creativity, and leadership
  • To develop skilled engineers with strong ethical values
