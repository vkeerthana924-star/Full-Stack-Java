Admissions

Join TPGIT Engineering College and build a strong foundation for your future career.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics from a recognized board.

  • 10+2 with PCM subjects
  • Recognized board certificate
  • Minimum passing percentage required

Application Process

  1. Obtain the application form from college office or website.
  2. Fill all personal and academic details correctly.
  3. Attach required documents.
  4. Submit the completed form at the admission office.

Important Dates

  • Application Start Date – 01 June 2026
  • Last Date to Apply – 30 June 2026
  • Counseling Date – 05 July 2026
  • Admission Confirmation – 10 July 2026

Documents Required

  • 10th Mark Sheet
  • 12th Mark Sheet
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Community Certificate
  • Aadhar Card Copy
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Counseling Allotment Letter

Admissions are based on merit and counseling process. Students must have passed 12th with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
